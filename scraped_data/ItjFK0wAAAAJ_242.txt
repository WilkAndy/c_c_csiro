Resumen: The effects of planting density on total DM production and distribution to storage roots were investigated over a 2 yr period (1975-77) at the U. of Queensland Research Station, Redland Bay, Australia. A random complete block exptl. design with 3 blocks was used with 2 harvests at 3 planting densities (5000, 10,000, and 20,000 plants/ha). Four plant samples were taken per plot. Harvests were 224 and 514 days after planting. Cv. M AUS 10 showed a plateau-type yield-density response curve over the 5000-20,000 plants/ha range.
