Measuring 12J\" by 10\", and containing 240 thick pages, this is a lavish book fit only for the stronger coffee tables, two-thirds photographs, one-third widely spaced text. The pictures, always beautiful, sometimes exciting, are almost all of animals which reside in parks, rather than views of the parks themselves.'Lovely photographs of animals in parks' is a fair working title for the book. Nevertheless the text is good. It goes through most of the major parks in the world, and manages to turn what could be largely indigestible dollops of fact about each into extremely readable prose.
